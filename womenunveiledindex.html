Women Unveiled – A Celebration of The Epiphany

Honoring National Women's Month

Introduction

In celebration of National Women's Month, "Women Unveiled – A Celebration of The Epiphany" seeks to honor women as the wellsprings of innovation, motivation, and creativity. Women are powerful beyond measure and brilliant beyond belief. Their minds revolutionize industries, their hearts fuel movements, and their spirits drive humanity forward.

About the Exhibit

Women are walking goldmines—repositories of wisdom, strength, and boundless potential. This exhibit unveils the extraordinary brilliance, resilience, and creativity that women embody. It celebrates the empowering epiphany that honors women's immeasurable contributions to expression, culture, and the future.

Event Details

Venue: Roc Bottom Studios

Dates: March 30th - April 20th

Opening Reception: March 30th, 2025 @ 1 PM - Featuring guest speakers, performances, and an artist panel.

Featured Themes

  • Explicit Empowerment: Celebrating the courage women hold to enhance the perception of their bodies.
  • Brilliance Beyond Belief: Highlighting women's intellectual, creative, and visionary contributions.
  • A Woman’s Touch: Showcasing women's experiences and expressions in masculine spaces and forms.
  • The Wealth of Womanhood: Depicting women as sources of wisdom, healing, and transformation.
  • Breaking Barriers: Showcasing trailblazing women paving new paths for progress.
  • She is Her: Recognizing the connection of women across different racial, economic, and social backgrounds.
